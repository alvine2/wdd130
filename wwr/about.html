Our History

A group of friends born in Jinja long enjoyed the charm of whitewater rafting on the river Nile which yielded a living and brought them new friends from the world over. Growing up, they took on the rapids of the Nile in jerry cans and some graduated to full time raft guides as the Nile became world famous. The friends have now come together to start Jinja Nile Rafting Uganda
